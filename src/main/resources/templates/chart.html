🧠 AI Model & Indicator Explainer

AI & Multi-Timeframe Engine

▾

Linear Regression & Random Forest models analyze 20 professional indicators via TA4J across 1D, 1W, and 1M timeframes to filter noise and reveal high-conviction trends.

  • Model Confidence — AI certainty score. Higher % indicates strong historical correlation with current patterns.
  • Data Samples — Volume of historical price points analyzed.

Wyckoff Market Structure

▾

Detects Accumulation, Markup, Distribution, and Markdown phases. Uses the Law of Effort vs Result (Volume vs Price Spread) and VWAP to identify institutional activity.

  • Accumulation/Markup (Cyan/Green) — Institutions are buying; suggests a BULLISH bias.
  • Distribution/Markdown (Orange/Red) — Institutions are selling; suggests a BEARISH bias.

Oscillators & Confirmation

▾
  • Trend/Momentum — Overall direction and speed of moves. Positive + uptrend = Bullish confirmation.
  • RSI Factor — Distance from neutral 50. Significant decimals (±0.20) highlight overbought/oversold extremes.
  • Indicators — MACD, BB, SMAs, and Volume Profile signals are cross-referenced to confirm cyclical turns.

Volatility & Money Flow

▾
  • Volatility (%) — Measures price variance (20p). Low (< 2%) suggests coiling; High (> 5-15%) suggests explosive moves and potential shakeouts (timeframe dependent).
  • Money Flow (MF) — Chaikin Money Flow tracks volume intensity. Positive (> 0): Aggressive buying; Negative (< 0): Aggressive selling.
